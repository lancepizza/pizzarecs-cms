About Pizza Recs

An opinionated guide to the pizzerias worth your time — and nothing else. Every place listed has been eaten at, thought about, and eaten at again. Organized by city. Rated without apology.

  • Ratings are on a sliding scale — a "Legit" pizza in Dallas might merely be "Worth the Stop" in New York.
  • Ratings also take into account the style and availability of that style in a given area.
  • I believe great pizza is about 41% dough, 36% quality of toppings, and 23% the balance between them.
  • Yes, I consider Chicago-style deep dish pizza.
  • Yes, I've eaten lots of pizza in Italy.
  • The reason it seems like you know your city better than me is that you probably do — unless you're in LA.
